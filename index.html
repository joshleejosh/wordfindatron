WORDFINDATRON generates random word find puzzles for you to solve. Play a while; play forever!

DRAG to circle words. UNDO to remove your last circle. CLEAR to start over. Hit RANDOM to create a new puzzle.

WORDEDITATRON can make puzzles just for you! Just enter a list of your favorite words, and let WORDEDITATRON take care of the rest.

ADD or REMOVE words from the list with the buttons. Set the GRID SIZE with the slider. Hit MAKE to create the puzzle. Hit QUIT to go back to just playing.
